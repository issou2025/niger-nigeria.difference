Culture & Languages

Niger and Nigeria are home to vibrant cultures and hundreds of languages.

  • Niger is a crossroads for Hausa, Zarma-Songhai, Tuareg, Fulani, and Kanuri cultures.
  • Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, including Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Fulani, and Kanuri.
  • Major languages in Niger: French (official), Hausa, Zarma, Tamajeq, Fulfulde, Kanuri.
  • Major languages in Nigeria: English (official), Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Fulfulde, Kanuri, Ibibio, Tiv, Edo, etc.
  • Both countries celebrate colorful festivals, music (Afrobeat, Hausa rap, Tuareg blues), and vibrant clothing styles.
